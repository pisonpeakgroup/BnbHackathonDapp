FAQs and Info

Here are some of the things people are asking about DAM Island!
What is DAM Island?

DAM Island is a platform of Dapps and games created for Binance Hackathon, currently hosting a game called "Crypto", and is expanding with more creations in the future!

The website is maintained by "MrDAM", and everything is made possible by the users.

DAM Island is designed to be a platform of longevity and friendly mutual growth. New Dapp and game ideas are always being explored! Got ideas? Join the Island Telegram chat, and feel free to share!

How can I connect?

To use DAM Island and its Dapps, you will need to use TronLink on a computer (via Chrome or Firefox), or Klever Wallet on an iPhone or Android device.

Please be sure to back up your keys and/or seeds - Nobody can help you recover your crypto, except you!

Crypto is a special kind of token that may not show correctly with some wallets. So long as you can see your Crypto on the website, your Crypto are safe and ready to be transferred, sold or HODLed on further!

Crypto Game

NOTICE: This game is a high-risk game, and you should only play with TRX you can afford to lose.


What is Crypto Game?

Crypto is an hourglass-style smart contract deployed on the blockchain, enabling users to hold "Crypto", earning BNB dividends via micro-economics within the Dapp.


How does it work?

Users buy and sell "Crypto" at a varying price, calculated on a number of factors including current Crypto supply. Other dapps and games can potentially feed the hourglass, too.


Does Compounding also get a 10% fee?

The Compound DAM of the Crypto game calls the buy DAM, so yes - 10% of your TRX Dividends will be distributed to Crypto holders when you compound.


What is Pricefloor?

Pricefloor is the contract which ensures that Crypto will NEVER be valueless - by method of holding Crypto like a regular player, Pricefloor will take the fall as "Last Player" if everyone decides to sell their Crypto!


How does Pricefloor work?

Pricefloor is a special Smart Contract which holds Crypto just like a regular player. It can never sell, transfer or destroy its Crypto - it can only buy more with the dividends it has earned, whenever someone "Raises the Floor".


Can I profit from Crypto?

There is possibility of earning TRX from holding Crypto over time - however, keep in mind that Crypto is a game, not an investment platform. Only spend TRX you can afford to lose - Crypto value is at the mercy of the holders!


Development

Is this Open Source?

The Smart Contracts of DAM Island are open source and verified on TronScan. You can view them any time, and of course build your own interface for them too!


Can I contribute?

Contributing is as easy as joining the Telegram chat, participating in the conversations and playing the games on DAM Island! 3rd party dapps are welcome to build on the contracts!


Future Plans?

DAM Island is intended to be a long-term project, hosting more than just Crypto. It will grow over time, however there are no certain steps of progression yet! Stay Tuned!
